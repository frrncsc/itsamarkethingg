Three 2nd-Year BSBA Marketing Major in Multimedia Arts Students on a Journey of Creativity and Innovation


Insights from Three Passionate BSBA Marketing Majors



In the vibrant and ever-evolving world of marketing, creativity and innovation are essential tools for success. Recognizing the importance of these qualities, three 2nd-year BSBA Marketing Major in Multimedia Arts students at FEU Diliman are embarking on a journey to make a positive impact in the field.


Meet Kahlil, Vele, and me, Hannah, we are three passionate and talented individuals who are driven by our love for marketing and multimedia design. Each of us brings a unique perspective to the table, contributing to a diverse and dynamic group of future marketing professionals. Kahlil is a natural-born storyteller, with a knack for capturing attention and engaging audiences through compelling narratives. Their passion for multimedia design shines through their ability to create visually appealing and engaging content that effectively communicates marketing messages. Vele is a strategic thinker, always looking for innovative ways to connect with consumers and achieve marketing goals. Their strong analytical skills and understanding of consumer behavior make them a valuable asset to any marketing team. I'm Hannah, I am a creative powerhouse, with a flair for developing fresh and original marketing concepts. Our ability to think outside the box and come up with unconventional ideas sets us apart from the crowd. Together, the three of us represent the future of marketing, a generation that is not afraid to challenge the status quo and embrace new technologies and trends. Our passion for creativity and innovation will undoubtedly lead us to success in our chosen field.


Our Journey in the BSBA Marketing Major in Multimedia Arts Program


We are BSBA Marketing Major in Multimedia Arts program at FEU Diliman that has provided Me, Kahlil, and Vele with a solid foundation in marketing principles, consumer behavior, and market research. We have also gained proficiency in graphic design, web design, and digital media production, giving them a well-rounded skillset that is in high demand in the marketing industry. The program's emphasis on hands-on learning has been particularly beneficial for us students. We have had the opportunity to apply our knowledge and skills to real-world projects, working with clients and organizations to develop effective marketing campaigns.


Navigating the Marketing Landscape


In the dynamic and ever-evolving world of marketing, the pursuit of knowledge and understanding is a continuous journey. As three 2nd year BSBA Marketing majors in Multimedia Arts, we're at the heart of this exciting field, eager to explore its depths, master its intricacies, and contribute our unique perspectives.


Beyond the Classroom: Unraveling the Marketing World


Our academic pursuits extend beyond the confines of the classroom. As active members of the Junior Marketing Association (JMA) of our university, we've had the privilege of organizing and participating in events that showcase the creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit of marketing students. These experiences have provided us with invaluable opportunities to network with industry professionals, hone our organizational skills, and gain practical insights into the real-world applications of marketing concepts. We've learned to think strategically, manage projects effectively, and collaborate with a diverse team of individuals.


Scholarship Opportunities: Paving the Way for Future Marketing Stars


Financial considerations can often be a barrier for aspiring marketing students. However, numerous scholarships and financial aid opportunities are available to support the academic pursuits of deserving individuals. During our time at university, we've witnessed firsthand the transformative impact of scholarships, enabling fellow students to focus on their studies and pursue their marketing dreams without financial constraints. We believe that access to education and opportunities should be equitable, and we're grateful for the support we've received to pursue our passions.


A Message to Aspiring Marketing Enthusiasts


The world of marketing is open to anyone with a passion for understanding and influencing human behavior. It's a field that rewards creativity, innovation, and the ability to think outside the box. If you're considering a career in marketing, we encourage you to explore its possibilities. Embrace the challenges, immerse yourself in the learning process, and don't be afraid to take risks and experiment. The marketing world awaits your unique talents and perspectives!



The Junior Marketing Association: A Platform for Growth and Connection


The Junior Marketing Association (JMA) is a student organization dedicated to fostering the growth and development of future marketing professionals. Through a dynamic calendar of events, workshops, and competitions, JMA provides students with opportunities to hone their skills, network with industry leaders, and gain valuable insights into the ever-evolving world of marketing.

Here are some of the key benefits of joining JMA: Skill Development: JMA offers a variety of workshops and seminars to help students develop their marketing skills in areas such as digital marketing, social media marketing, and event planning. Networking Opportunities: JMA events provide students with opportunities to connect with industry professionals, build relationships, and gain mentorship from experienced marketers. Industry Insights: JMA invites guest speakers and industry experts to share their insights on the latest trends and developments in the marketing landscape. Career Advancement: JMA participation can enhance a student's resume and make them more competitive in the job market.
